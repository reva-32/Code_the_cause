# EduLift

**EduLift** is an AI-powered, multilingual educational platform designed to support NGO and under-resourced students through personalized learning, inclusive accessibility features, and continuous academic support — even in low-connectivity environments.

> **Our Core Belief**: Education should adapt to the student, not the other way around.

---

## Problem Statement

NGO-supported students often face systemic challenges that hinder effective learning:

* One-size-fits-all teaching methods
* Limited access to personal mentors
* Poor or inconsistent internet connectivity
* Use of low-end or shared devices
* Lack of inclusive tools for specially-abled learners

These challenges frequently lead to:

* Learning gaps
* Reduced confidence
* Higher risk of student dropouts

---

## Our Solution — EduLift

EduLift addresses these challenges by offering:

* Personalized learning paths based on real performance
* AI-powered doubt resolution available anytime
* Inclusive features for differently-abled learners
* Offline and low-bandwidth support for rural and under-resourced centers

## Key Features

### Adaptive Learning Engine

* Placement tests to assess the initial learning level
* Subject-wise level allocation (different level for each subject)
* Topic-level assessments to validate understanding
* Lessons unlock gradually based on performance
* Strong fundamentals ensured before progression

### AI Tutor (Groq Powered)

* Instant chatbot-based doubt resolution
* Contextual explanations linked to current topics
* Reduces dependency on continuous human mentoring

### Inclusive & Accessible Learning

* High-contrast UI mode
* Audio-based lessons and explanations
* Voice assistance for visually challenged students
* Simplified visuals for students with special learning needs

### Multimedia Learning Support

* Video lessons
* Audio lessons
* Downloadable notes for no-internet situations
* Low-quality video options for slow networks

### Gamified Learning Experience

* Learning streaks to encourage consistency
* Achievement badges to boost motivation

### Offline & Low-Connectivity Support

* Downloadable lessons and notes
* Lightweight, optimized UI for low-RAM devices
* Designed for rural and low-bandwidth environments

## Learning Rules & Assessment Logic

* Topic summary **must be submitted** before attempting a topic test
* Topic test unlocks only after summary submission
* **3-times fail logic** applied:

  * 1st & 2nd failure: auto-adjusted difficulty / revision support
  * 3rd failure: guardian and admin are notified for intervention
* Exams conducted **every 3 months**
* Hard copy of exam papers to be submitted to the nearest partnered school

## Dashboards Overview

### Student Dashboard

  * Lessons (video/audio/text)
  * Downloadable notes
  * AI doubt chatbot
  * Topic summaries & tests
  * Hobby Hub
  * Health care forms

---

### Guardian Dashboard

* Add, edit, and delete student records
* View detailed student profiles and reports
* Track real-time student progress
* Identify weak areas early
* Record visitor details (entry & exit time)
* View and act on health care form results
* Personally intervene in a child’s issues
* Maintain health records:

  * Vaccination details
  * Disability information (if any)
  * Height & weight (recorded every 3 months)
    
* College tie-ups for Class 10 students (all subjects)
* View student academic progress
* Download and print exam question papers

---

* Centralized system control

* Monitor visitor logs
* Upload and manage learning content
* Upload test papers for printing
* Collect feedback from guardians

* Ensure platform quality, security, and scalability

* **Database**: MongoDB
* **Authentication**: JWT
* **AI Engine**: Groq, LangChain

---

## System Flow

1. Student begins with a placement test
2. System assigns subject-wise personalized levels
3. Lessons unlock based on performance
4. Student submits topic summary
5. Topic test validates understanding
6. AI tutor assists with doubts and concept reinforcement
7. Progress is tracked and visualized on NGO & Admin dashboards
